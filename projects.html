Ongoing Projects

Eco- Friendly Event-making

Provide assistance with event planning services. This covers accommodations, printing, catering, venue rentals, and logistics. The service will be offered in accordance with green standards, which call for the use of eco-friendly practices and resources.

Digitalisation Services

Provide IT services to rural and less literate population using tailored technology solutions. Connecting farmers and remote sites to centralized hub for access to critical information regarding weather, climate, natural disaster and events.

Gender Empowerment

Creating local women's groups to produce aid items and handicrafts aimed at boosting income for Tajik women who are vulnerable to domestic abuse. Displaying a true representation of rural women's daily lives along predetermined tourist routes in Tajikistan's countryside.

Rurism - The Tajik Experience

Offer tourism packaged for a rural experience. Traditional life, less accessible areas and parts of Tajikistan. Historical background, religious history, traditional manufacturing, lifestyle, weddings and funerals. Everything to open up the culture to a tourist in a safe and accessible way.

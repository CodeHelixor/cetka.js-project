CONTACT FORM – SMTP (FreeHosting.com) SETUP

1. In FreeHosting cPanel: create or confirm email account info@cetka.tj and note its password.

2. Install PHPMailer on your PC in the site folder:
   - Open terminal in the html folder (e.g. c:\Users\HomePC\Downloads\compare\html).
   - Run: composer require phpmailer/phpmailer
   - Upload the whole site including the "vendor" folder to FreeHosting.

3. On the server: open send-mail.php and set the password:
   - Find: $MAIL_PASSWORD = '$1234Song';
   - Replace with the real info@cetka.tj mailbox password and save.

4. Upload these files to FreeHosting:
   - send-mail.php
   - contact.html (updated form)
   - js/main.js (updated)
   - vendor/ (folder from Composer)

5. Test: open your contact page, fill the form, submit. You should get a “Thank you” message and receive the email at info@cetka.tj.

Troubleshooting:
- If it fails, check contact.html?sent=0&err=... in the address bar for the error.
- Ensure SMTP is allowed and the password is correct in cPanel webmail.
